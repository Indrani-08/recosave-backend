Help & Support

If you have any issues, please check the following FAQs:

Q: Why are my schemes disappearing?

A: Schemes are saved permanently to our database. If they disappear, ensure your Flask server is running and you have successfully logged in.

Q: Why do I see an 'AI Analysis Error'?

A: The AI needs complete data (Age, Salary, Investment Goal) to run. Please update all fields on the Profile screen.

Q: How do I change my profile details?

A: Use the "Edit Profile" button on the Dashboard. The form pre-fills with your current data.
